### 📑 Professional PDF Export
High-fidelity, print-ready A4 PDFs (4.5× scale, multi-page). **4 layout templates** (Modern / Classic / Bold / Compact) · custom accent colors · fonts (Inter / Roboto / Lato / Poppins) · watermarks (Draft / Paid) · company logo.

### 🧮 Smart Invoice Lines
Drag & drop reorder · duplicate in one click · per-line tax rates & discounts · item catalog (50 items) · units: hours, days, km, flat rate…

### 💰 Discounts, Deposits & Tax
Global or per-line discounts (% or fixed) · deposit/advance with auto-calculated balance · multi-rate tax breakdown.

</td>
<td width="50%">

### 👥 Client Management & History
Save clients locally · auto-save every downloaded PDF · Draft / Sent / Paid status · search & filter · duplicate number warnings.

### 🔗 Share & Export
PDF download · share-by-link (gzip compressed) · pre-filled mailto · JSON import/export (`.iloveinvoice`) · CSV history export.

### 🎨 Modern Interface
Live A4 preview · 5-step stepper with live total · dark mode · keyboard shortcuts (`Ctrl+D` PDF, `Ctrl+Enter` add line, `Ctrl+S` export) · WCAG 2.1 AA accessible · guided onboarding tour for first-time users.

## 🚀 Getting Started

**Use online** (recommended):

> **[uolav.github.io/iloveinvoice](https://uolav.github.io/iloveinvoice/)** — works instantly, no install.

**Self-host:**

```bash
git clone https://github.com/uolaV/iloveinvoice.git
cd iloveinvoice
open index.html   # or: npx serve .
```

No build step. No `npm install`. Zero configuration.

<br>

## 🏗️ Architecture

```
iloveinvoice/
├── index.html      → Structure, forms, modals
├── style.css       → Custom styles (floating labels, stepper, A4 preview…)
├── countries.js    → 64 country definitions with embedded i18n
├── app.js          → All logic — state, rendering, PDF, formatters
└── sw.js           → Service Worker for offline support
```

**Stack:** Vanilla JS · Tailwind CSS (CDN) · html2pdf.js · pako · Google Fonts

<br>

## 🔒 Privacy & Data

All invoice and client data is stored **exclusively in your browser's localStorage**. Nothing is ever sent to a server.

| Service | Purpose | When |
|:--|:--|:--|
| `flagcdn.com` | Country flag images | When rendering the country selector |
| CDN (Google Fonts, Tailwind, html2pdf) | Static assets | On page load |

## ❓ FAQ

<details>
<summary><strong>Is iloveinvoice really free?</strong></summary>
<br>
Yes. 100% free, open-source (MIT), no premium plan, no feature limits, no ads. Forever.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Where is my data stored?</strong></summary>
<br>
Entirely in your browser's localStorage. Your invoices, clients, and settings never leave your device. There is no server, no database, no account.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I use it offline?</strong></summary>
<br>
Yes. After your first visit, the Service Worker caches all assets. You can create and download invoices without an internet connection.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Does it work on mobile?</strong></summary>
<br>
Yes. The interface is fully responsive with a mobile-optimized layout, touch-friendly controls, and a scaled A4 preview.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I add my company logo?</strong></summary>
<br>
Click the logo upload area in the Issuer section. Your logo is stored as base64 and appears in both the live preview and the exported PDF.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I share an invoice without generating a PDF?</strong></summary>
<br>
Yes. Use the Share button to copy a link that contains the full invoice state. The recipient sees a read-only preview and can download the PDF themselves.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Is it legally compliant for my country?</strong></summary>
<br>
iloveinvoice includes tax rates, late-payment mentions, and professional ID formats for 64 countries. Always verify compliance with your local regulations.
</details>
